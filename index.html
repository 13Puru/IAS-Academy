Welcome To AJMAL IAS Academy

Founded in 2023, AJMALS IAS Academy has been consistently delivering impactful training, boasting the highest student success rate in UPSC, APSC, and ACS examinations. UPSC, APSC & ACS.

To drive meaningful progress in our nation's development, we need dedicated individuals equipped with strong administrative skills to serve society effectively. Our mission is to cultivate highly skilled and responsible officers who uphold the principles of Administrative Law in India. Recognizing the immense responsibilities of an IAS officer and the intense competition of the field, we are committed to providing the finest training and guidance for aspirants to succeed by providing UPSC and APSC coaching to our students. Our branch is located in Hojai, Assam.
